A small toolkit of NLP building blocks for Persian, targeting the
morphological and orthographic quirks that make Farsi awkward for
generic multilingual pipelines.

- **Tokenizer** — handles zero-width non-joiner boundaries and
  clitics correctly.
- **Lemmatizer** — reduces inflected forms back to their citation
  form (verb stems in particular).
- **POS tagger** — trained on the Bijankhan corpus, with a small
  augmentation set for social-media Persian.
- **Dependency parser** — transition-based, trained on the Persian
  Dependency Treebank.
